# 🚀 Quick Start Guide - AI-Powered ATS

## ⚡ Get Started in 3 Steps

### Step 1: Open the Application
```
Simply open index.html in your web browser
```

### Step 2: Choose Your Path
- **Are you a job seeker?** → Click "Apply for Job"
- **Are you a recruiter?** → Click "Recruiter View"
- **Want to check status?** → Click "Candidate Login"

### Step 3: Explore!
Follow the intuitive interface and experience the AI recruitment workflow.

---

## 👤 For Job Seekers

### Your 5-Minute Journey

**1. Fill the Application Form (2 min)**
   - Enter your personal details
   - Upload your resume (any PDF/DOC file)
   - List your skills (e.g., "JavaScript, Python, React")
   - Select your experience level
   - Click Submit

**2. Watch AI Analyze Your Resume (10 sec)**
   - See the progress bars fill up
   - View extracted skills, education, experience
   - Review the parsed information

**3. Complete the AI Interview (2 min)**
   - Answer 6 questions from the AI recruiter
   - Mix of multiple choice and text responses
   - Topics: career goals, role preference, salary, availability
   - Natural conversation flow

**4. View Your Scores (1 min)**
   - See your compatibility metrics:
     - Skill Match percentage
     - Role Compatibility score
     - Resume Quality rating
     - ATS Optimization level
   - Read detailed analysis
   - Review strengths and recommendations

**5. Submit to Recruiter (5 sec)**
   - Click "Submit to Recruiter"
   - Receive your Application ID
   - Done! 🎉

---

## 👔 For Recruiters

### Your 3-Minute Setup

**1. Open Recruiter Dashboard (5 sec)**
   - Click "Recruiter View" from landing page
   - See empty dashboard with stats

**2. Generate Sample Candidates (5 sec)**
   - Click "Generate Sample Data" button
   - Instantly see 10 realistic candidates
   - View dashboard statistics update

**3. Explore Candidate Management (2 min)**
   - **Search**: Type names, skills, or locations
   - **Filter**: Select status (Applied/Shortlisted/etc.)
   - **Sort**: By score, date, or name
   - **View**: Click any candidate card
   - **Act**: Shortlist or schedule interviews

## 🔧 Troubleshooting

### Common Issues

**Problem**: Resume upload doesn't work
- **Solution**: Use PDF or DOC files only, under 5MB

**Problem**: Application form won't submit
- **Solution**: Fill all required fields (marked with *)

**Problem**: Candidates not showing in dashboard
- **Solution**: Generate sample data or submit an application first

**Problem**: Animations not smooth
- **Solution**: Use Chrome, Firefox, or Edge for best performance

**Problem**: Data disappeared after closing browser
- **Solution**: Data is saved in localStorage - check browser settings allow local storage

## 📊 Understanding Scores

### What the Metrics Mean

**Skill Match (75-95%)**
- How well your skills match job requirements
- Based on skill keywords and experience level

**Role Compatibility (70-90%)**
- Fit for the specific role type
- Considers career goals and preferences

**Resume Quality (80-95%)**
- Professional presentation
- Completeness of information
- ATS-friendly format

**ATS Optimization (70-95%)**
- Keyword optimization
- Format compatibility
- Parsing success rate

**Overall Score**
- Average of all four metrics
- Used for candidate ranking
